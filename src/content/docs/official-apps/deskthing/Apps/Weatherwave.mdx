Developed by Dammit Jeff, Weatherwave is an attractive weather, clock, and spotify display. To install it, simply download the application from the **Downloads** tab in the DeskThing Server, see [Managing Apps](/official-apps/deskthing/managing-apps).

Adjust the units and location in the app settings.
